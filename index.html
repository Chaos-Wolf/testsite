Welcome to L & M Auto, Inc. and Papa's Healthy Food & Fuel, located in the Beautiful Berkshires of Western Massachusetts. Conveniently located on Rt. 23, in East Otis, MA we have been serving the needs of Berkshire County visitors and residents alike, for over 30 years. Whether you are a first time visitor, returning visitor, or one of the many year round residents of the Berkshires, please make it a point to stop by.

From our great selection of organic items, including fruits & vegetables, skin care products, delicious ice cream, fresh coffee and yes ...even dog food to our homemade soups, salads & dressings.

We pride ourselves on friendly service, freshness and quality ...such as our homemade bake goods, sandwiches "voted best in town" and our Deli featuring quality Boar's Head Meats and Cheeses.

Our selection of organic foods is one of the best in the area. Papa's stocks a variety of organic groceries including coffee, pasta, soup, sugar, cereal, chocolate, health products and much more.

Papa's has dedicated time and energy into sourcing and supporting our local farmers and vendors in the region, stocking their wares year round. A small sampling such as local honey, maple syrup and candies, micro-green salads, soaps and lotions can be found in stock and make great gifts!
